Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 1, 2024

This Privacy Policy applies to the following websites: mohdasif.com (hosted on Tumblr), mohdasif.com/blog (hosted on Blogger), and other associated pages hosted on Cloudflare and GitHub Pages.

1. Introduction

We at mohdasif.com are committed to protecting the privacy of our visitors. This Privacy Policy outlines our practices concerning the collection, use, and sharing of your information through the use of our websites.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect any personal data from visitors to our websites. This includes, but is not limited to, names, addresses, email addresses, and payment information.

3. Third-Party Policies

Our websites are hosted on Tumblr, Blogger, Cloudflare Pages, and GitHub Pages, which are governed by their respective privacy policies. We do not control these platforms' data collection and processing practices. For more information, please refer to the following privacy policies:

  • Tumblr's Privacy Policy
  • Blogger's Privacy Policy
  • Cloudflare Privacy Policy
  • GitHub Pages' Privacy Statement

4. Cookies

We do not personally use cookies on our website, but third-party services like Google may use cookies to enhance your browsing experience. We use a script to disable the cookies banner to provide an uninterrupted browsing experience. By using our website, you acknowledge and accept this approach to managing cookies. For detailed information regarding the cookies, we encourage you to consult the Google cookies policy.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via email at privacy@mohdasif.com.

Please note that while we do not collect personal data, the platforms hosting our websites may collect data according to their policies. It's important to review and understand their terms and ensure your privacy policy is compliant with their requirements. Additionally, if our circumstances change and we begin to collect personal data, we will update our privacy policy accordingly.
